# Figma AI Prompt v1 — China Travel Hero Prototype

Create a high-fidelity Figma prototype for the **hero section of a tourism landing page**. This is only the first screen, not the full website.

## Product goal

The page is for overseas travelers and overseas Chinese users who are interested in traveling to China but have not decided where to go yet. The business goal is lead generation: encourage visitors to leave their contact information, preferred destinations, travel dates, group size, and budget so a travel advisor can follow up.

## Core concept

Design the hero as a **China destination exploration tool**, not a generic travel agency landing page.

The central visual should be an interactive-feeling **map of China** with destination markers and real travel photo thumbnails. The map should inspire users to explore China and make them feel: “I want to go there, and it seems easy to ask for help planning.”

## Layout

Use a **map-centered layout**.

Suggested structure:

- Top navigation with brand name, simple links, language switch, and a small WhatsApp/contact CTA.
- Hero headline above or overlapping the map.
- Large China map in the center as the main visual anchor.
- Destination markers around the map, each with a small real-photo thumbnail and label.
- A lightweight consultation form as a floating card near the lower right or bottom center.
- Small trust badges beneath the form or near the bottom of the hero.

Do not use a traditional left-text-right-image layout. Do not use a full-screen photo carousel. The map should be the star.

## Destinations to show

Feature natural, high-desire China destinations:

- Zhangjiajie
- Guilin
- Jiuzhaigou
- Yunnan

Optional supporting destinations if space allows:

- Huangshan
- Chengdu

The destination photos should feel real, immersive, and travel-inspiring. Prioritize natural scenery, with small touches of culture or local life. Avoid fake stock-photo stickers or generic smiling-tourist cutouts.

## Visual style

Style direction: **friendly professional city exploration tool**.

It should feel:

- Friendly and easy to approach
- Professional enough to trust
- Modern and clear
- Travel-inspiring
- Interactive and exploratory
- Not overly luxury or cold

Avoid:

- Generic SaaS dashboard look
- Overused glassmorphism
- Cheap travel agency poster style
- Heavy red/yellow promotional graphics
- Too much information in the first screen
- Abstract AI illustrations
- Large template gradient cards

Use warm, bright, clean colors. A light paper or soft off-white background is preferred. Accent colors may include warm red, ink dark, soft gold, and natural green/blue from photos.

## Copy direction

Use bilingual English + Chinese copy. English should be primary, Chinese can be secondary.

Tone: friendly, relaxed, like a local friend who is also a professional travel advisor.

Main headline example:

**Not sure where to go in China?**  
**Tell us what you love — we’ll help you plan it.**

Chinese support line:

**还没想好去中国哪里？告诉我们你喜欢什么，我们帮你定制行程。**

Supporting copy:

“Pick a destination, share your travel idea, and get free help from a local China travel advisor.”

## CTA

Primary CTA:

- **Free Trip Consultation**
- **免费咨询 / 定制行程**

Secondary CTA:

- **Get My China Travel Plan**
- **WhatsApp Us**

The CTA should feel low-pressure. Make it clear that users do not need a fixed plan yet.

## Consultation form

Create a compact floating form card in the hero.

Title:

**Start planning your China trip**  
**开始定制你的中国行程**

Fields:

- Name
- WhatsApp / Email / WeChat
- Where do you want to go?
- Travel time
- Group size
- Budget range

Add helper text:

“No fixed plan yet? That’s totally fine.”  
“还没确定路线也没关系，我们可以先聊聊。”

Submit button:

**Get Free Consultation**

## Trust elements

Add 3–4 small trust badges or short claims:

- Local China travel advisors
- Free consultation, no obligation
- Help with routes, hotels, transport & tickets
- Bilingual support for overseas travelers

These should be subtle and credible, not loud marketing claims.

## Prototype state

Create one high-fidelity desktop hero frame, around 1440 × 900 or 1440 × 960.

If possible, include a selected marker state on the map, such as Zhangjiajie selected, with a small destination preview card showing:

- Destination photo
- Destination name
- 1-line reason to visit
- Small action: “Add to my trip”

## Design quality bar

The result should look like a real modern travel website hero, not a wireframe. It should be suitable for client review as a first visual direction. Prioritize composition, hierarchy, realistic photo use, and lead-generation clarity.
